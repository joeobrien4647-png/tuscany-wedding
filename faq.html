FAQ

Everything you need to know

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the RSVP deadline? +

Please RSVP by 1st March 2027. This gives us time to finalise numbers with our vendors. If your circumstances change after you've RSVP'd, please let us know as soon as possible.

What's the dress code? +

For the ceremony and reception on Saturday, the dress code is Formal / Black Tie Optional. Think suits and cocktail dresses or formal gowns.

For the welcome drinks on Friday and farewell brunch on Sunday, Smart Casual is perfect.

Top tip: Ladies, consider wedges or block heels for the garden ceremony. The grounds are beautiful but grassy!

Can I bring my children? +

While we love your little ones, our wedding will be an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives you the chance to have a proper holiday and let your hair down!

If you need help finding childcare options in Tuscany, please get in touch and we can share some recommendations.

What's the weather like in May? +

Late May in Tuscany is typically warm and sunny with temperatures around 20-25°C (68-77°F). Perfect wedding weather!

We recommend bringing sunglasses, sunscreen, and a light layer for the evening when it can get a bit cooler.

Do I need to speak Italian? +

Not at all! English is widely spoken in tourist areas of Tuscany. That said, learning a few basic phrases like "Grazie" (thank you) and "Buongiorno" (good morning) is always appreciated by locals.

Can I take photos during the ceremony? +

We kindly ask for an unplugged ceremony. Please put your phones and cameras away during the ceremony so you can be fully present with us. Our photographer will capture all the special moments.

During the reception, feel free to snap away!

Will there be vegetarian/vegan options? +

Absolutely! Tuscan cuisine is wonderful for vegetarians, and our caterers can accommodate vegan and other dietary requirements. Please let us know your needs when you RSVP.

Is there parking at the venue? +

Yes! La Conca has ample free parking for guests. If you're staying at nearby accommodation, many places are within walking distance of the venue.

What about gifts? +

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for, especially as you're travelling all the way to Tuscany!

However, if you'd like to give a gift, we've set up a honeymoon fund to help us create amazing memories on our post-wedding adventure. See the section below for details.

I have more questions! +

No problem! We're happy to help. Just drop us an email at wedding@sophieandjoe.com and we'll get back to you.

Accessibility Information

We want all our guests to feel comfortable and included. Here's what you need to know about accessibility at the venue.

♿

Wheelchair Access

The main reception and dining areas are accessible on one level. The ceremony space in the olive grove has a paved pathway. Please contact us if you need specific accommodations.

🚗

Parking

Accessible parking spaces are available close to the main entrance. Please let us know in advance if you require a reserved spot.

🍽

Dietary Needs

Our caterers can accommodate all dietary requirements including allergies, intolerances, and preferences. Please specify when you RSVP.

  • Vegetarian & Vegan options
  • Gluten-free available
  • Nut-free on request
  • Halal/Kosher - please ask
🔊

Hearing & Visual

If you have hearing or visual requirements, please let us know so we can ensure you're seated appropriately during the ceremony and speeches.

🩹

Medical Needs

There's a quiet room available if you need a break. The nearest hospital is Ospedale della Valdichiana (20 min drive). Please bring any necessary medications.

🚸

Terrain

The venue is a working farm with some uneven ground. We recommend:

  • Wedge heels over stilettos
  • Comfortable walking shoes for exploring
  • Contact us for specific route guidance

Need specific accommodations? Please get in touch!
