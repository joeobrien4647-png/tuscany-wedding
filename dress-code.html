Dress Code Guide

Everything you need to know about dressing for our Tuscan celebration - from welcome drinks to farewell brunch.

Friday Evening

Welcome Drinks by the Pool

🌴 Smart Casual

Ladies

  • 👗 Sundress or midi dress
  • 👗 Linen trousers & nice top
  • 👗 Jumpsuit
  • 👗 Wedges or sandals

Gents

  • 👔 Chinos & polo/shirt
  • 👔 Linen trousers & shirt
  • 👔 Smart shorts okay!
  • 👔 Loafers or smart trainers

Ceremony & Reception

  • 👗 Block heels recommended

Gents

  • 👔 Suit (tie optional)
  • 👔 Dinner jacket welcome
  • 👔 Dress shirt
  • 👔 Smart dress shoes

💡 Top Tips

👟

Mind the Grass

The ceremony is in the olive grove on grass. Ladies, block heels or wedges will be much kinder than stilettos!

🌞

Tuscan Weather

Late May is warm (20-25Â°C). Bring a light layer for the evening when it cools down.

👓

Sun Protection

The ceremony starts at 3:30pm. Consider sunglasses and don't forget SPF!

💃

Dancing Shoes

We'll be partying until late! Bring comfy shoes to change into for the dance floor.

The Do's & Don'ts

👍 Please Do

  • Wear colours - we love them!
  • Dress comfortably for the heat
  • Bring layers for the evening
  • Wear practical shoes for grass
  • Express your personal style
  • Bring sunglasses

👎 Please Avoid

  • All white/ivory (bride's territory!)
  • Anything too casual for Saturday
  • Very high stilettos (grass + heels = disaster)
  • Anything you can't dance in
  • Heavy fabrics (it'll be warm!)
