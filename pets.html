How We Found Each Other

"We weren't looking for a dog. We went 'just to look' at the rescue centre. Three hours later, we were buying a bed, toys, and had our hearts completely stolen."
- Sophie & Joe

Biscuit was found as a stray and brought to a local rescue. The moment he put his paw on Sophie's knee, we knew he was coming home with us. He's been the best addition to our little family - and the reason we can never have a lie-in anymore!
