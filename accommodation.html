Booking Tips

Book Early

May is peak season in Tuscany. We strongly recommend booking your accommodation as soon as you know you can attend.

Consider Sharing

Renting a villa with friends can be great value and a wonderful experience. Many have pools and gorgeous gardens.

Stay Longer

If you can, extend your trip! Explore Florence, Siena, or the Chianti wine region. We're happy to share our favourite spots.
